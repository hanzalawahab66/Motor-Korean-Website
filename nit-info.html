Autos NSM Inspection Technology (NIT)

Brief guide to Autos NSM Inspection Technology (NIT) service.

Three features of NIT service

Check the condition of your used car with Autos NSM!

115‑point inspection
A detailed 115‑point inspection checks exterior condition, options, operational status, and accident history.
Detailed photos & videos
Photos and videos of exterior, interior, and engine compartment provide vivid verification of condition.
Convenient access
Mobile‑optimized report accessible anytime, available in English, Arabic, and Spanish.

Autos NSM Inspection Technology

The exterior condition of the vehicle is assessed, including the overall paint finish, bumpers, and other components.

Autos NSM inspection process

01 Request for Inspection
Request NIT services through Autos NSM staff.
02 Inspection preparation
Bring the vehicle to Autos NSM's yard at Incheon Port, or an inspection expert visits the storage yard.
03 Vehicle inspection
115 points including exterior, underbody, engine compartment, and options.
04 Vehicle photography
Detailed photos and videos captured for thorough verification.
05 Report issuance
Mobile‑optimized report issued; viewable on the vehicle's details page.

Eligible Vehicles for Inspection

Supported
Compact cars, sedans, SUVs, and trucks under 1.4 tons.
Not eligible
Electric vehicles, buses, large trucks, and heavy equipment.

Important notes regarding NIT

NIT does not predict the future condition of the vehicle or guarantee its quality; it provides information to help buyers clearly understand the vehicle's current condition.
Due to the service being conducted while stationary, functions that require driving cannot be assessed.
Non‑inspectable items include odometer tampering, flood damage, and options that can only be verified during road operation.
